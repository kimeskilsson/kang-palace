About us

Kang Palace is an old fashioned restaurant with a history that goes back as far as the early 1900. By bringing the best chefs from China over to America and paying them more than well, we insured a good and strong foundation to stand on.
We are proud to say that we still ensure to have the best quality ingredients and the best chefs at our disposal, making it possible to bring the best and nothing else to our customers.

So if you ever plan a visit to San Francisco and want to visit Kang Palace. Be sure to make a reservation in good time.
